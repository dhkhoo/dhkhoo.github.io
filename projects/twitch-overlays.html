Twitch Overlays

Graphic Design

Under the pandemic, gaming has becoming a large pass-time for many as a way to connect and meet new people on the internet. Streaming games and creating content on Twitch has been a passtime which many of my friends have picked up over the past couple months. A friend of mine reached out for assistance in making overlays for their Twitch stream and I was able to create these simple, yet aesthetically pleasing animated overlays for their viewers.
