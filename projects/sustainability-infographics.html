Sustainability Infographics

Graphic Design

Purpose

Sustainability has always been a passion of mine and I have been advocating the importance of enviorment since I've been in high school. And through that passion, I've found that there's not enough education which is done to promote sustainability and there exists a lot of misinformation about the topic. As a visual learner, I find that being able to see information helps a lot in learning and understanding topics, and though these infographics, I hope that I'm able to help others understand sustainability and the enviorment as well.

Role

Graphic Designer
