About Me

Hey! I'm Skylar, or as my friends call me, Sky! I'm a just your normal gal, except that fact that instead of traveling for a hobby, it's become the dream job that I never though I'd have a chance to do! I just recently started this blog, to record all of my travel adventures and mishaps, for anyone who's curious enough to read!

I've been traveling full-time since June 2019 and though my plans have been cut a bit short due to the current pandemic, I've been to 18 countries and plan to travel to more as soon as it's safer to head out there again! Until then, I'm planning sharing all of my past adventures through this blog, so make sure to keep an eye our for new posts!

Until next time,

Sky
